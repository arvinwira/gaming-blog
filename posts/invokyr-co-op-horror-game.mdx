## Gameplay Overview

In *Invokyr*, you explore a sprawling abandoned house with four players online. Your goal is to reach the end of a cursed board game but each dice roll summons a new threat. You may uncover walking carnivorous plants, scuttling mannequins, or nightmarish traps that force you to flee, hide, or fight.

Key gameplay elements include:

- **Dice rolls**: each triggers a unique horror creature or trap.

- **Hidden dice**: scattered throughout the house and each can only be used once.

- **Special dice effects**: healing, hiding, hindering pursuers, risky but strategic.

- **Permadeath and betrayal**: players can choose to help or abandon teammates.

- **Shifting house biomes**: jungle, ice mountain, oversized toy chest, and more.

The tension builds with every decision, roll or wait, cooperate or hesitate, survive or succumb.

The story begins in a dusty, forgotten board game from the 1970s placed in an eerie mansion. Once beloved, that game is now under the control of the sinister Gamemaster, voiced by Amelia Tyler (who also appeared in *Baldur’s Gate 3* and *Hades 2*). The game traps players inside and forces them to play for their freedom. Escape is possible but only if you're willing to roll and face what lurks in every shadow.

&nbsp;

As you explore, you uncover the sinister history of the game and the house itself. The narrative slowly reveals dark secrets with each run, encouraging replay for deeper lore and new unlockable rewards.

## What Makes It Unique

*Invokyr* combines elements rarely seen together in one game:

- **Board game mechanics in survival horror**: The dice based progression adds unpredictability and a compelling risk reward structure.

- **Psychological tension and co‑op betrayal**: You may trust teammates or leave them behind when chances are slim.

- **Dynamic house biomes**: Each run throws you into a new twisted environment full of visual flair and hidden danger.

- **Permanent consequences**: Special one use dice, permadeath, and shifting objectives keep every session fresh.

It’s a fresh take on co‑op horror where strategy, psychology, and luck collide.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is *Invokyr*?

*Invokyr* is a 4 player cooperative psychological horror game developed by Ludogram. It blends classic board game mechanics with survival horror in a haunted house setting, where each dice roll determines what horrors the players face.

### When is *Invokyr* coming out?

As of now, *Invokyr* is still in development and does not have an official release date. It is available to wishlist on Steam, with more updates expected from the developer soon.

### Can I play *Invokyr* solo?

*Invokyr* is primarily designed as a multiplayer co-op experience for up to four players. There has been no confirmation of a single player mode, though this may change in future updates.

### Is there local co-op or split screen?

Currently, *Invokyr* is planned as an online multiplayer title. There has been no official confirmation of local co-op or split screen functionality at this time.

### What platforms will *Invokyr* be available on?

So far, *Invokyr* has only been confirmed for release on PC via Steam. No announcements have been made regarding console releases.

### Who voices the Gamemaster in *Invokyr*?

The sinister Gamemaster is voiced by Amelia Tyler, known for her roles in *Baldur’s Gate 3* and *Hades 2*. Her performance brings a haunting theatricality to the in game narrator.
